dazzling sunsets to melt your heart

King Neptune rests at the heart of the oceanfront. Selfie contest March 23. Prizes for the most unique selfie. Come on down and let your creative side run wild.

Adam's rock and roll golf tournament March 2nd and 3rd. $50 entry fee which includes an innova disc from Discmania and a T-shirt. Tee off starts at 9 am Saturday for the pro tour. and 10 am on Sunday for the family disc toss-off. Food vendors on-site. Come on down to Bayville Park and let's play some disc golf.

Come down every Friday for family movie night! Come and grab your blankets and lawn chairs. Popcorn will be provided free of charge. Food trucks will be on-site to offer a variety of food to host your taste buds. Come down early; the movie starts at 7 pm. Hope to see you there!

April 4-6 The Virginia Beach Melody Club will be hosting their annual sandcastle tournament. Entry fee is $35 per group, and the winner will be crowned victor that Saturday evening. Proceeds will go to autism awareness foundation. Come and enjoy this family fun event!

Virginia Beach Convention Center. A host of activities on any given weekend. This month is muscle car month. Every weekend the VBCC will be hosting different muscle cars and trucks from around the country. Tickets are $15 per person at the door or $12 at your local Riley Auto Parts store.
