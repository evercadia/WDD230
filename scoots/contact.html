Scoots has two conveniently located rental facilities on the beautiful island of Cozumel, Mexico. Our primary location is situated near the bustling Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in the heart of el Centro de Cozumel. Easily accessible from the ferry terminal, this central location serves as the perfect starting point for exploring the island's vibrant streets and attractions.

Additionally, we have a secondary rental facility strategically positioned near the Terminal Puerta Maya, catering specifically to cruise line passengers and patrons visiting Cozumel for the day. Located further south along the coast, this location provides easy access to the island's pristine beaches, scenic vistas, and adventurous excursions.

Both of our rental facilities offer a wide range of friendly and hassle-free rental services, including motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs, ensuring that our customers can explore Cozumel's wonders with ease and convenience.
